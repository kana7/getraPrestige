WELCOME

GETRA PRESTIGE provides property and lifestyle services to private and corporate clients, we take your stress away and make it ours.

Whatever your needs are, we can assist you and will make your life as easy and enjoyable as possible.

GETRA PRESTIGE will assist homeowners with the management and maintenance of their Luxembourg properties whether they live here full time or only a few weeks a year. Our services enable our clients to concentrate on important things, such as family or work. Whilst we devote our attention to ensuring the home is being run smoothly. We react to alarms service the boiler, make sure the property is secure and much more.
